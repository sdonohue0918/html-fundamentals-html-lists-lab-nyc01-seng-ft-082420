Kellye's Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepperjack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
    1. spread butter on bread and pan
    2. place bread in frying pan and fry
    3. add cheese on top of bread
    4. cover with second slide of bread
    5. turn over and fry for 2 minutes
